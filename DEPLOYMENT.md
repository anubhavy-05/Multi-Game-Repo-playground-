## Steps to Enable GitHub Pages

### 1. Go to Repository Settings
1. Visit your repository: https://github.com/anubhavy-05/Multi-Game-Repo-playground-
2. Click on **Settings** tab (top right)

### 2. Enable GitHub Pages
1. In the left sidebar, click on **Pages**
2. Under **Source**, select:
   - Branch: `main`
   - Folder: `/ (root)`
3. Click **Save**

### 3. Wait for Deployment (1-2 minutes)
GitHub will automatically deploy your site. You'll see a message:
> Your site is ready to be published at https://anubhavy-05.github.io/Multi-Game-Repo-playground-/

### 4. Access Your Live Website
Once deployed, visit:
- **Main Page:** https://anubhavy-05.github.io/Multi-Game-Repo-playground-/
- **Snake & Ladder:** https://anubhavy-05.github.io/Multi-Game-Repo-playground-/snake-ladder/
